Bunlit Meadows

Poetry by an unclear quantity of rabbits

Bunny nibbles, skittering, scampering
Gone without a trace
Poof, dash, whoosh

The soft howl of a moonlit wind
Intangible, yet everpresent
Shiver, deafening, silent

Impossibly tall stalks of grass
Higher than the sky, lower than the ground
growing, growing, grown

Mossy meadows and rolling hills
Soft like pillows, old as time
Shifting, shaking, settling

Marshmallows dotting the skies
Brilliant white and deepest blue
Drifting, dreaming, dusk till dawn

A simple cabin in tune with nature
A gentle family there to love
Calm, comfortable, cozy
